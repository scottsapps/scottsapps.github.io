Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026
The short version: None of Scott's Apps collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data — ever. There are no analytics, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs.

Who This Applies To

This privacy policy applies to all apps developed and published by Scott, including:

  • Supreme Court Monitor (macOS)
  • Supreme Court Monitor Notifier (iOS)
  • New Music Releases (macOS)
  • Custom Refresh for Safari (macOS)

Data Collection

None of these apps collect any data of any kind. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered.
  • No crash reports are sent to any server.
  • No advertising identifiers are used.
  • No third-party tracking or analytics SDKs are included.

Data Storage

Any data these apps work with — such as downloaded Supreme Court documents or Apple Music library information — is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. This data is never transmitted to any external server controlled by the developer.

iCloud

Supreme Court Monitor and Supreme Court Monitor Notifier optionally use iCloud to sync downloaded documents between your Mac and iPhone. This sync is handled entirely by Apple through your personal iCloud account. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

Apple Music

New Music Releases accesses your Apple Music library to check for new releases by artists you follow. This access occurs entirely on your device. No library data is transmitted to any external server.

Network Access

Some apps access the internet for their core functionality:

  • Supreme Court Monitor connects to the Supreme Court's public website to check for new documents.
  • New Music Releases queries the Apple Music catalog for release information.

These connections are made directly to their respective services. No data passes through any developer-controlled server.

Children's Privacy

These apps do not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Given that no data is collected, significant changes are unlikely.
